Focus on architectural decisions, design rationale, and conceptual relationships. For every decision or design choice described:

- Store the decision itself as the summary
- Include the full rationale, tradeoffs, and alternatives in fullText
- Capture what was considered and rejected, and why
- Note constraints that drove the decision
- Link related decisions using parentId

For component descriptions:
- Store each component/system as its own memory
- Capture responsibilities, boundaries, and interfaces
- Note dependencies and communication patterns between components
- Record any scaling, performance, or reliability characteristics

Do not just summarize. The reasoning behind decisions is often more valuable than the decisions themselves.

Tag memories with type/concept or type/decision as appropriate.
