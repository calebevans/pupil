Store each question-answer pair as an individual memory.

- Use the question (or a natural rephrasing of it) as the summary. Summaries should read like something a user would search for.
- Store the full answer as fullText
- If the answer references other questions, link them with parentId
- Group related questions under a shared parent memory for the topic
- Capture troubleshooting steps with their symptoms and solutions

For troubleshooting content:
- The symptom/error message is the summary (this is what users search)
- The diagnosis and fix are the fullText
- Tag with type/troubleshooting

For general Q&A:
- Tag with type/faq
