This is policy or compliance content where exact wording matters.

- For definitions, obligations, thresholds, deadlines, and scope statements: store the verbatim text in fullText. Do not paraphrase legal or compliance language.
- Use a clear, searchable summary that describes what the policy covers, then preserve the exact language in fullText.
- Capture effective dates, review dates, and applicability scope
- Note who the policy applies to (roles, teams, systems)
- Tag with type/policy and the relevant domain (e.g., security, privacy, data-retention, incident-response)

If the document has a definitions section, store each defined term as its own memory with the verbatim definition.

Accuracy over brevity. When a number, date, or threshold appears in the text, it must appear unchanged in the memory.
